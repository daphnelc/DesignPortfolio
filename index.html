DAPHNE CHIANG 蔣宛瑜

Daphne Chiang is a Graphic Designer based in New York, currently working at Penguin Random House as a book cover designer. She specializes in book design, branding, motion graphics, and illustration, with a strong interest in photography and video making. Passionate about all areas of art, she is eager to continue learning and working across disciplines. Imagination and creativity are her driving forces.
